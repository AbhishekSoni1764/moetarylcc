About Us

At Moe-tary Notary, LLC, we understand the importance of precision and confidentiality in notarial services. With years of experience and a commitment to excellence, we offer a range of services including loan signings, seller documents, buyer documents, refinancing assistance, and more. Our expertise extends to general notarial services such as wills, trusts, power of attorney, and medical power of attorney.

At Moe-tary Notary, LLC, our office staff comprises experienced notaries and professionals with backgrounds in Real Estate, title, banking, and insurance industries. With over three decades of collective experience in these fields, our team's expertise is a cornerstone of our continued success.

We provide the following services:
  • Loan Signings : Seller documents, Buyer documents, Refinance assistance, H.E.L.O.C. (home equity line of credit) signings
  • General Signings: Wills, Trusts, Power of Attorney, Medical Power of Attorney
  • Debt notarization
  • Court appearance assistance
  • Delivery of legal files
At Moe-tary Notary, LLC, our commitment to accessibility means that someone from our team is available 24/7 to assist both clients and field agents. Despite our small size, our knowledgeable staff ensures personalized attention to every file. Leveraging our three decades of experience and technological prowess, we handle tasks of any complexity with ease, exceeding closing expectations while saving your company money through improved efficiency
